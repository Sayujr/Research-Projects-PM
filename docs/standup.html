Standup & check-in

A short conversation with Claude. Morning: what's ahead. Evening: what happened. Claude acts as your PM — asks, adjusts, logs.

Morning standup

Every day Claude opens with: today's 3 items, overdue flags, calendar shape, and any anti-burnout signals. Auto-fires via the SessionStart hook. You can skip, edit, or add — the conversation adapts.

Why a conversation, not a form

A form asks you to remember what matters. A conversation asks about what Claude already knows — "Figure 4 slipped yesterday. Still blocked, or unblocked now?" You only type the short answer.

What Claude logs from this chat

  • Overnight changes → projects/*.md
  • Confirmed plan → week-plans/2026-W17.md
  • Wins you report → progress/2026-04.md
  • Time data → meta/task-history.json
  • New blockers → projects/*.md + Slack alert
This is what Claude would do in a real Claude Code session. In the live demo, the messages are scripted to show the flow. In your actual repo, Claude produces the structure from .claude/commands/standup.md.
